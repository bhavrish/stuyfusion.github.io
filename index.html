About:

We are one of the Stuy Fusion 479, one of the FTC teams in Stuyveant High School, NYC. We participate in the First Tech Challenge, while trying to help spread STEM, as well. In addition, in our own team, we have a lot of newbies. Thus, we help promote confidence, hard-work, and persistence, qualities that will make the members of our team, the best that they can be.
